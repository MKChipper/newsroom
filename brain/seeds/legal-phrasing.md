# Legal phrasing table (template)

Save into the brain as slug `legal-phrasing`, kind `legal`. The legal desk
applies these swaps mechanically. Add every pattern you learn.

| Risky pattern | Safe phrasing |
|---|---|
| "banned by the regulator" | "the ASA ruled the claims misleading" |
| "renaming to dodge scrutiny" | "same formulation, new label" |
| "no proof of what's inside" | "no public COAs or assay results found" |
| "scam" / "fake" | state the specific gap: dose, evidence, price |
| any statement of intent ("they know…") | state what was done; let the reader conclude |

General principles the desk already applies without this doc:
- Named brands require a sourced claim with citation. No receipt, no name.
- Regulator-neutral verdict language only.
- No medical advice in either direction.
