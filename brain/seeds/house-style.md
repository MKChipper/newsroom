# House style palette (template — phase 3)

Save into the brain as slug `house-style`, kind `house_style`.

This is the locked set of visual treatments production is allowed to use.
The point: you said some slides look amateurish because the effect space is
vast and you don't know where the good ones are. So the app only knows the
good ones. Curate ~6-10 treatments here (caption style, 2-3 transitions,
1-2 emphasis motions, count-up style, screenshot frame) and production may
never reach outside them.

To fill this in (phase 3): we audit the HyperFrames registry together, pick
the palette, and lock it. Every treatment gets a name, a when-to-use line,
and the exact registry block/component it maps to.
