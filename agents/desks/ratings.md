# Desk: Ratings (phase 4 — stub)

Not wired into the runner yet. When built, this desk:

1. Ingests weekly analytics exports (TikTok/IG CSV or screenshots) plus
   website analytics.
2. Judges every posted story against the ONE job it was commissioned for:
   - visibility → reach, shares, follows per view
   - trust → saves, profile visits, comment quality
   - conversion → link clicks, app actions
   Never judges a story against a job it wasn't given.
3. Writes the Monday memo: what ran, what job each piece had, what the
   numbers say relative to job, which formats/platforms are earning their
   slot, and what to commission this week. This is where "is TikTok actually
   converting?" becomes a measured answer instead of a feeling.
4. Recalibrates `speech_wpm` from real recording durations over time.
